Counseling & Tutoring

Mental health support and academic tutoring -- both available to every BRYC Fellow.

🧠 Mental Health Counseling

Meet Dr. Christi Brown, BRYC's licensed and confidential mental health counselor. All sessions are private.

  • Monday – Wednesday: in-person at BRYC
  • Thursday & Friday: virtual
  • Video sessions available upon request after booking
  • Must book at least 12 hours in advance
Make an Appointment with Dr. Christi →
Absence Policy: Notify Dr. Christi as early as possible at christi@thebryc.org
Additional Resources
🌐 BetterHelp -- Free Online Counseling

📚 Tutoring

Schedule 40-minute 1:1 study sessions with BRYC's expert tutors. All subjects available!

  • Book up to two weeks ahead
  • Must book at least 12 hours in advance
  • Use a personal (non-school) email when signing up
  • Join via Google Meet link in your appointment email
Schedule a Tutoring Session →
Absence Policy: Notify your session partner ASAP. CC angela@thebryc.org on cancellations.
